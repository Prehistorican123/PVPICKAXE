The Basic Blacksmith will be your first blacksmith you will encounter. This blacksmith gives you basic tools from Cobble Rank all the way to Gold Rank. The tools that you may get from him mostly consist of iron and basic leather.

The Merchant at spawn gives you basic things such as golden apples, different stews and in general miscellaneous things. But it can also trade between gold and silver coins but also with a tax. The Merchant takes away 2 silver coins every time you trade.

The Fletcher at spawn gives you low to mid-tier bows, and crossbows. The weakest one being the normal bow, and the strongest being the silver bow. He also has different types of arrows which you can use. This includes slowness, poison, and weakness.

The Expert Blacksmith is the next step after the Basic Blacksmith. He provides better tools all the way to Diamond Rank down to Emerald Rank. This is the midway between the basic, and the legendary blacksmith. You can find him near the cave entrance.

The Book Trader can be found at spawn in the small cave below the big tree. He trades books for silver or gold coins, and the books only go up to the third level (Example: Efficiency III)

The Ore Dealer is important, it trades your hard grinded ores for silver or gold coins. He can trade cobblestone all the way to obsidian blocks that are found in the skymines. He can be found in The Cave.

The Ranked Ore Dealer is exclusively available for the people who purchased a rank. He gives you better deals on the low to high mid-tier ores. But high-tier ores are kept the same price.

The Mine Teleporter is also quite important, it can teleport you to the mine you'd want to go to, but you must be a certain level to go to certain mines. (Example: If you have iron armor but you want to go to the skymines, you are can't go.)
